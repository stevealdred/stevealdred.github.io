Charting

The purpose of this chart was to look at item sales per caps to determine if adding Happy Hour pricing had any impact on overall revenue.

The chart illustrates item per caps prior to and after the introduction of Happy Hour pricing and allows the user to quickly see that Happy Hour pricing has increased the number of items that an individual purchases which leads to additional revenue.
